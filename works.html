üñ§ Reflection and Takeaway

This was my first ever UX project, I gained valuable insights into fundamental app development processes through the course. While the structured steps aligned with the real-world app life cycle, I recognized a need for increased interaction and collaborative brainstorming. Limited to my own thoughts at times, I now appreciate the potential richness that diverse perspectives bring to design. In future projects, I am eager to collaborate within teams, fostering a dynamic environment that allows for more detailed, innovative, and well-rounded product designs.
